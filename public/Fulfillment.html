Cancellation Policy

Once a Subscription Plan is purchased, it cannot be cancelled or downgraded during the active subscription period. Subscribers may choose to upgrade their Subscription Plan at any time during the subscription period. The cost to upgrade will be calculated based on the difference between the new plan's price for the remaining period and the per-day price of the current plan multiplied by the days left.

If an upgrade is performed, any unused Credits under the current plan will be cancelled, and new Credits will be issued upon successful upgrade completion. Subscribers must ensure sufficient Credits or funds are available to complete any upgrades, as failure to do so may result in suspension of membership privileges.
